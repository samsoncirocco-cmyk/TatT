# TatTester Customer Journey Map

This comprehensive journey map tracks the experience of first-time tattoo seekers as they navigate from initial discovery through post-booking stages. The map specifically addresses the unique anxieties and decision-making challenges faced by users who want meaningful tattoo designs but fear commitment. Each stage identifies key touchpoints, pain points, and opportunities for TatTester to reduce friction and build confidence through AI-powered design tools, AR visualization, and smart artist matching.

---

**Key Insights:**
- **Commitment anxiety peaks** at stages 3-4 (design creation/visualization) and stage 7 (booking) - these require strongest friction reduction
- **Trust building** is critical throughout - social proof, transparency, and professional validation reduce anxiety
- **Seamless handoff** from digital design to artist contact (stage 6-7) is make-or-break moment
- **Post-booking engagement** creates referral loops and repeat usage for future tattoos
