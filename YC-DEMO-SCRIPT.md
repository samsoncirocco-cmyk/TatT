## Anticipated Q&A

**"How do you monetize?"**
> Three revenue streams: (1) SaaS subscription for the design studio, (2) booking transaction fees when users connect with artists (3) premium artist profiles/promotion. The booking fee is the wedge — it's a natural payment moment.

**"What about Midjourney / DALL-E?"**
> General image generators don't understand tattoo constraints — skin tone interaction, wrap anatomy, ink aging, style-specific line weights. And none of them connect to a real artist. We're vertical-specific end-to-end.

**"How do you acquire artists?"**
> Artists want qualified leads who already know what they want. We don't compete with their Instagram — we send them clients who've already designed, previewed, and are ready to book. That's a pull, not a push.

**"What's your CAC look like?"**
> Tattoo intent is highly searchable ("tattoo ideas," "tattoo design" — billions of annual searches). Content-led SEO plus a free tier for design iteration gets users in. Conversion happens at the booking moment.

**"Why now?"**
> Three tailwinds: (1) Image generation quality crossed the quality bar for tattoo-specific output in 2025, (2) AR on mobile is now native and fast, (3) consumers are comfortable using AI for creative decisions. Two years ago, none of those were true.
